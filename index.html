ABOUT US
HISTORY
“ Strange story of the emergence of healthy coffee ”
Serving over 1.5 million coffee lovers, even as we continue to expand and seek to source the best coffee across the globe. But what keeps us going? It’s what we’d like to call a ‘coffee-high’, and some truly passionate folks who’re on this mission with us.
OUR MISSION
“ It's one of those days where even my coffee needs coffee ”
A great cup of coffee just doesn’t happen! It requires love, passion, dedication and hard work at every stage. From nurturing of right coffee plants under best coffee growing conditions to picking, drying, curing, roasting and brewing.
OUR STORY
“ Coffee is a beverage that puts one to sleep when not drank ”
We all seek connection, with each other, with ourselves. This quest leads us to express ourselves in many different ways. For us, a good satiating meal and coffee that hits the note became a medium of such a connection.
